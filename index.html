Who Me?

My name is Nathan Linerode. I am a professional junior web designer and developer.

Canton, OH born and raised, relocated to Charlotte, NC. My life has taken new direction since moving to North Carolina. A drive and passion to continuously learn web technologies and languages, coupled by meeting many web developers of my caliber, has pushed me to new heights.

Responsive & Mobile Driven Design

Throuhout my process of studying and learning web development, a passion has grown in me for what I see as an inevitable future in the field.


Responsive and mobile web design.

From my own personal experience, I have noticed over the years the growing trend of using mobile devices to explore the Internet. Whether it be searching for a local business or restaurant, to searching for a job, mapping out a route and even reading the news, more and more often are mobile devices the primary medium.


UI/UX For The Masses

I find much excitement in engaging this medium and taking on the "mobile-first" approach in all of my projects. Whenever I find websites on the daily that are not very mobile friendly, I am constantly motivated to create a better user-experience in my own projects. Always searching for the best solution to a mobile-first platform is a challenge, but is overly rewarding.
